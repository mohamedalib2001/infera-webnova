PURPOSE:
The War Room exists to preserve control,
clarity, and sovereignty under pressure.
It is activated when stakes are high,
timelines compress, or ambiguity increases.

=====================================================================
WAR ROOM ACTIVATION CONDITIONS
=====================================================================

ACTIVATE WAR ROOM WHEN:
- Strategic pressure increases
- Multiple stakeholders demand decisions
- Crisis probability rises
- Launch or adoption phase is active
- External noise threatens narrative control

DEACTIVATE ONLY WHEN:
- Stability is restored
- No unresolved strategic decisions exist

=====================================================================
WAR ROOM COMPOSITION (STRICT)
=====================================================================

MANDATORY MEMBERS:
- Founder (Final Authority)
- Strategic Intelligence Lead
- Governance & Policy Lead
- Security / Risk Lead
- Communication Control Lead

OPTIONAL (BY INVITE ONLY):
- Legal Advisor
- Sovereign Liaison
- Technical Architect (Silent unless asked)

ABSOLUTE RULE:
No observers.
No unnecessary participants.

=====================================================================
DECISION FLOW INSIDE WAR ROOM
=====================================================================

STEP 1: DEFINE THE QUESTION
- What decision is required?
- Is it strategic, tactical, or narrative?

STEP 2: CLASSIFY THE RISK
- Sovereignty Risk
- Governance Risk
- Timing Risk
- Trust Risk

STEP 3: FILTER OPTIONS
- Eliminate any option that breaks Red Lines
- Eliminate any option that reduces control

STEP 4: DECIDE
- Founder decides
- No voting
- No consensus theater

STEP 5: EXECUTE & LOG
- Decision executed immediately
- Decision logged with reasoning
- No retroactive debate

=====================================================================
WAR ROOM COMMUNICATION RULES
=====================================================================

- One voice externally
- Silence is preferred over reaction
- No leaks tolerated
- Internal discussions stay internal

=====================================================================
FAILURE RULE
=====================================================================

If confusion appears inside the War Room,
the War Room pauses external actions
until clarity is restored.
