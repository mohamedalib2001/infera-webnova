PRINCIPLE:
In crisis, silence is a tool.
Words are deployed only when they increase control.

=====================================================================
CRISIS LEVELS
=====================================================================

LEVEL 1: INTERNAL CONFUSION
Examples:
- Misunderstanding
- Internal disagreement
- Minor leaks

SPOKESPERSON:
- NONE

ACTION:
- Internal clarification only
- No external communication

MESSAGE:
- Silence

=====================================================================

LEVEL 2: INVESTOR / BOARD CONCERN
Examples:
- Doubts
- Pressure for speed
- Strategic anxiety

SPOKESPERSON:
- Founder or Board Chair ONLY

MESSAGE FRAME:
- Calm
- Long-term
- Control-focused

KEY PHRASE STYLE:
"We are exactly where we planned to be."

=====================================================================

LEVEL 3: GOVERNMENT QUESTIONING
Examples:
- Sovereignty concerns
- Control questions
- Security clarification

SPOKESPERSON:
- Sovereign Liaison ONLY

MESSAGE FRAME:
- Assurance
- Stability
- Control retention

KEY PHRASE STYLE:
"Full authority remains national at all times."

=====================================================================

LEVEL 4: MEDIA / PUBLIC NOISE
Examples:
- Speculation
- Inaccurate reporting
- External narratives

SPOKESPERSON:
- NONE (by default)

ACTION:
- No reaction
- No correction unless strategic

MESSAGE:
- Silence is preferred

=====================================================================

LEVEL 5: DIRECT ATTACK / ACCUSATION
Examples:
- Security allegations
- Political pressure
- Coordinated narrative attack

SPOKESPERSON:
- Founder OR designated sovereign authority

MESSAGE FRAME:
- Factual
- Minimal
- Non-defensive

KEY RULE:
Respond once.
Never debate.
Never escalate.

=====================================================================
ABSOLUTE COMMUNICATION RULES
=====================================================================

- No emotional responses
- No reactive statements
- No over-explaining
- No unscheduled interviews
- No anonymous sources

SILENCE IS ALWAYS ACCEPTABLE.
LOSS OF CONTROL IS NOT.
